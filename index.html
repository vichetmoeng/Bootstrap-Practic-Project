What is Matcha ?

Matcha Green Tea, hand-picked and granite ground into fine powder, is popular for its potent health benefits and versatility, derived from consuming 100% of the tea leaf. Matcha also has about half the amount of caffeine as coffee, and is extremely rich in antioxidants.
